## What NannyKeeper does

NannyKeeper is payroll software for families who employ a nanny, housekeeper, senior caregiver, or other household employee. It runs payroll, computes federal and state taxes, generates pay stubs and W-2s, and produces a Schedule H worksheet for your federal 1040 — without the $80–150/month price tag of a full-service company.

Same math. Lower cost. You handle the (very simple) submission step.

## What's included

- **Payroll for any pay frequency** — weekly, biweekly, semimonthly, or monthly. Federal income tax, Social Security, Medicare, FUTA, state income tax, state unemployment (SUTA), SDI, PFL, and local taxes (county/school district, OH RITA & JEDD, KY occupational, NY DBL/PFL, NJ TDB/FLI).
- **Optional direct deposit** via Stripe Connect ACH. Funds arrive in ~2 business days.
- **State-aware pay stubs** — emailed PDFs every payroll, with the right line-item labels for each state.
- **Quarterly tax tracking** — FICA, FUTA, SUTA liabilities, with reminders before filing deadlines.

- **Time tracking** — magic-link timesheets the nanny fills from any phone.
- **Nanny contracts** — drafted, e-signed, stored.
- **EIN concierge** — we file your SS-4 with the IRS by fax (Plus Annual). Free DIY guidance otherwise.
- **Multi-employer / nanny shares** — separate employers, separate runs, one W-2 per family per nanny.

## FAQ

**Do I need to pay nanny taxes?**
If you pay any one household employee $3,000 or more in 2026 (the federal FICA threshold), you owe employer Social Security and Medicare. FUTA kicks in at $1,000/quarter aggregate across all household employees. State thresholds are often lower — California $750/quarter, DC and NY $500/quarter.

**What's Schedule H?**
The IRS form household employers attach to their personal Form 1040 to report and pay federal employment taxes for the year. NannyKeeper produces a fully-completed Schedule H worksheet you can copy onto the form yourself or hand to your CPA.

**Does NannyKeeper file taxes for me?**
No. We compute liabilities, generate W-2s, and produce a Schedule H worksheet — but you (or your CPA) handle the actual submission to the IRS, your state, or via your 1040.

**How do I run nanny payroll with NannyKeeper?**
Add your nanny's profile, set their pay rate, and click "Run payroll." We generate the paycheck, calculate every tax, send a pay stub, and (if you've enabled it) initiate direct deposit. End-to-end for one biweekly run takes under a minute.

**How does NannyKeeper compare to Poppins, HomePay, GTM, or SurePayroll?**
Same calculations. Much lower price. Full-service services typically run $80–150/month and submit filings on your behalf. NannyKeeper is DIY at $10–18/month — the math is identical, you just hand-submit. Best if you want accuracy without the concierge premium.

**Do you support nanny shares?**
Yes — each family is a separate employer with its own payroll runs. The nanny gets one W-2 per family at year-end.

**Does my nanny need a W-2?**
If you paid them $3,000+ in 2026 (FICA threshold) and treated them as a household employee, yes. NannyKeeper generates W-2s automatically if you ran payroll all year, or via the W-2-Only plan ($10) if you DIY'd.

**Do you have an API?**
Yes. Free key, 19 endpoints, OpenAPI spec, MCP tools. Docs at [nannykeeper.com/developers/reference](https://www.nannykeeper.com/developers/reference).
